The Urban Outlaw Supply Philosophy

Raw.
Honest.
Unapologetic.

Laser-etched raw kraft cover with that industrial aesthetic – no glossy finish, no fancy coating. Just honest materials that show their character from day one. Each cover is cut from unbleached cardstock and run through our laser, burning the design deep into the fibre. No two covers are identical. The natural variations in the kraft – the flecks, the tone shifts, the raw edges – aren't flaws. They're proof it's real.

The torn-edge black band isn't decoration; it's attitude. The UOS badge isn't printed on – it's burned in.
