The Build

Custom lasered Kraft notebooks that live in your pocket, survive your day, and earn their scars.

"These notebooks aren't made to look pristine. They're not meant to stay pristine. Built for the streets, workshops, and your EDC where ideas get dirty and work gets done."

The Raw Utility Journal

Built Different

— Built Rough

Laser-etched raw kraft cover with that industrial aesthetic – no glossy finish, no fancy coating. Just honest materials that show their character from day one. Each cover is cut from unbleached cardstock and run through our laser, burning the design deep into the fibre. No two covers are identical.

— Burned In

The torn-edge black band isn't decoration; it's attitude. The UOS badge isn't printed on – it's burned in. The natural variations in the kraft – the flecks, the tone shifts, the raw edges – aren't flaws. They're proof it's real.

— Made For Makers

Inside you'll find space for the chaos: Creator Identity fields for when you need to remember whose notebook this is after it's been through hell. Objectives section for mapping out the next move. Lined pages that don't care if you're sketching with pencil dust or taking notes in the rain.

— Designed in the UK

Born in Portsmouth. Designed with 42 years of obsessive perfectionism. Every element is intentional, functional, and built to last. This isn't mass-produced generic stationery – it's a tool for people who actually use their notebooks.

The Specs

  • Size A6 (105 × 148mm)
  • Cover Raw Kraft Cardstock
  • Pages Lined / Cream Stock
  • Finish Laser-Etched
  • Band Torn-Edge Black
  • Origin Designed in UK

What's Inside

Creator Identity page – Name, contact, the basics. For when your notebook outlasts your memory of where you left it.

Objectives section – Map out what you're working towards. Goals, targets, the next move.

Lined pages – Clean, simple, ready for whatever you throw at them. Sketches, notes, plans, chaos.

Start & End dates – Track when this chapter of work began and when it was filled.
