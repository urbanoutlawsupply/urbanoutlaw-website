The Build

— Built Rough

Laser-etched raw kraft cover with that industrial aesthetic – no glossy finish, no fancy coating. Just honest materials that show their character from day one. The torn-edge black band isn't decoration; it's attitude. The UOS badge isn't printed on – it's burned in.

— Made For Makers

Inside you'll find space for the chaos: Creator Identity fields for when you need to remember whose notebook this is after it's been through hell. Objectives section for mapping out the next move. Lined pages that don't care if you're sketching with pencil dust or taking notes in the rain.
